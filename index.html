About Jan

Ahoj and welcome. Thank you for taking an interest in my work. As you might have guessed, wood is my craft.

I grew up in the Czech Republic, where I began working with wood as a young boy. I was inspired by my father, who is a unique innovator himself. I loved seeing the different creations being brought to life, and that’s where it all began.

I graduated from a technical school specializing in wood manufacturing. After school, I worked in mass production, shipbuilding, state design, residential and commercial design, and project management.

Currently, I work in New York as an art-oriented designer and consultant while implementing projects.

Some of my clients include:

  • Czech National Theatre, Director- Robert Wilson, Architect-Christian Wassmann
  • Frames Kremen, Designer Zachary Lind, CEO-Valerio Morabito
  • Zalla Studio, JWBuilt, Ltd Scenografie
  • Pantarhei-Yachts.cz

My CV is available upon request.
